# Secure the Job: Breaking into Security

A podcast series that highlights how security work can be exciting for new employees, regardless of their original career path.  

**Guidelines**  

All mentions: *Secure the Job: Breaking into Security*  

Note the italicized font.  

Prepositions in titles—*into* in this example—are usually lowercase (along with articles and conjunctions).  

Titles of individual podcast episodes should be in quotes, while the title of the ongoing group or series of podcasts should be italicized.
