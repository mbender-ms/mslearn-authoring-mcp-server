# Small Business Community (Microsoft 365)

Microsoft Small Business Community is an online community for small business owners and employees who are often not highly technical to engage, learn, and get the most out of their Microsoft 365 products and services. Small Business Community provides an inviting, nonjudgmental platform to engage with Microsoft and other community members.

**Guidelines**

- First mention: Microsoft Small Business Community
- Subsequent mentions: Small Business Community
