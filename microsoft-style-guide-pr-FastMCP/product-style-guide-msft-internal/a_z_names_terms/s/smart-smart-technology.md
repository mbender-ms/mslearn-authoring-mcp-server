# smart, smart technology

Don’t use.

The term *smart* is outdated and inadvertently attributes human qualities to technology. *Smart* is heavily associated with devices (smart home, smart phone, etc.), and doesn’t necessarily convey AI. Applying smart to features and software could obscure the benefit of the feature.

Microsoft's customer research discovered that *smart technology* is also overused. The impression is that it represents technologies that are ubiquitous and with limited abilities to connect and provide access, requiring the user to do more.
