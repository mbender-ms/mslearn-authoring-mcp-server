# cubic foot, cu ft

The plural is *cubic feet.*  

The abbreviation for both the singular and plural is *cu ft* without periods. But avoid using the abbreviation unless space is limited.

**Examples**  
1 cubic foot  
32 cubic feet  
1 cu ft  
32 cu ft
