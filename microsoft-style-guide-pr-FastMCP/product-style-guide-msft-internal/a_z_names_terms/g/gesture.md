# gesture

To move your fingers on a touchscreen or make a motion intended to be tracked by sensors in Xbox or mixed reality.

Specific gestures include:

- tap
- double-tap
- tap and hold
- pan
- flick
- swipe
- pinch
- stretch
- bloom

For detailed information about referring to these gestures, see [Describing alternative input methods](/style-guide/procedures-instructions/describing-alternative-input-methods) in the Microsoft Writing Style Guide.

**Example**  
When we explain how to do certain things on your Surface, we sometimes mention the gestures you can use.
