# Demographics language: How to talk about groups of people

This section provides guidance on how to talk about the statistical characteristics and identities within human populations, such as race, ethnicity, sex, gender, age, disabilities, and socioeconomic, residential, and legal status. Note, this style guide recognizes that language of identity is fluid and dynamic, especially with the growing recognition of [intersectional](~\responsible-ai-style-guide\fairness\experiences\intersectionality.md) identities.
