# Make audiences aware there is no single definition of fairness that applies to all AI systems

Make audiences aware there is no single definition of fairness that applies to all AI systems. Definitions for fairness may vary, depending on the use-case context. Further, even for the same definition there might be several different ways to measure or operationalize that definition of fairness.Â
